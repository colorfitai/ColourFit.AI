Privacy Policy

How ColorFit AI collects, uses and protects your information

Last Updated: March 2025

Updated with new AI features

Welcome to ColorFit AI — your smart personal stylist. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our app at colourfitai.vercel.app. By using our app, you agree to this policy.

1 Information We Process

ColorFit AI processes the following data locally in your browser to provide style suggestions:

  • Age, height, weight and gender — for body type analysis
  • Skin tone HEX color code — optional, for color palette matching
  • Occasion preference — for outfit selection
  • Camera / Photo NEW — used only to detect skin tone, processed instantly in your browser, never uploaded to any server
  • AI Chat messages NEW — your style questions sent to Claude AI API for responses

All body measurements and skin tone data are processed only in your browser. They are never stored, transmitted or saved anywhere outside your device.

2 Camera & Photo Access NEW

ColorFit AI can access your camera or photo gallery — but only when you choose to use the skin tone detection feature.

  • Camera is accessed only when you tap "Open Camera" button
  • Photos are processed instantly in your browser using JavaScript Canvas API
  • No images or photos are ever uploaded to our servers
  • No photos are stored or saved anywhere
  • Camera access stops immediately when you capture or close
  • You can skip camera access entirely and enter skin tone manually

Your camera and photos stay 100% private. We only read the average color from the center of the image to detect skin tone — nothing else.

3 AI Chat (Claude AI) NEW

ColorFit AI uses Claude AI by Anthropic to power the AI Style Advice and AI Chat features.

  • When you use AI Chat or get AI style advice, your profile data and messages are sent to Anthropic's Claude API
  • This includes: gender, age, body type, skin tone type, occasion and your chat messages
  • Anthropic processes this data to generate style responses
  • Chat history is stored only in your current browser session — it clears when you close the tab
  • We do not permanently store your chat messages

By using AI Chat, your style questions are processed by Anthropic (Claude AI). Please review Anthropic's Privacy Policy at anthropic.com/privacy

4 App Installation (PWA) NEW

ColorFit AI is a Progressive Web App (PWA) that can be installed on your device like a native app.

  • When installed, a service worker caches basic app files for offline use
  • Cached data includes only app code — no personal data is cached
  • Dark mode preference is saved to your browser's localStorage
  • You can uninstall the app anytime from your device settings
  • Clearing browser data removes all cached app files

5 Google AdSense & Cookies

Our website uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to serve personalized ads.

  • Google may use the DoubleClick cookie to serve relevant ads
  • Third-party vendors may use cookies based on your prior visits
  • Opt out of personalized ads at Google Ad Settings
  • Google Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy

6 Third-Party Services

ColorFit AI uses these third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Anthropic (Claude AI) — AI style advice and chat responses
  • Unsplash — Fashion outfit images loaded from Unsplash CDN
  • Google Fonts — Syne and DM Sans typography
  • Google AdSense — Advertisements
  • Vercel — Website hosting and deployment
  • GitHub — Code repository and backup hosting

7 Data Storage & Security

ColorFit AI has no backend server or database of its own. Here is what gets stored where:

  • Your device only: Dark mode preference (localStorage)
  • Browser session only: AI chat history (clears on tab close)
  • Never stored anywhere: Your photos, camera images, body measurements
  • Anthropic servers: AI chat messages (processed and not retained per their policy)

No accounts. No sign-ups. No databases. Your personal style data never leaves your device.

8 Children's Privacy

ColorFit AI is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child is using our app, please contact us.

9 Your Rights & Controls

  • Opt out of Google ads via Ad Settings
  • Clear browser data to remove all cached app files
  • Deny camera access in your browser settings
  • Skip AI features entirely — app works without AI chat
  • Use private/incognito mode for full privacy
  • Uninstall the PWA anytime from device settings

10 Changes to This Policy

We update this Privacy Policy when new features are added. The "Last Updated" date at the top reflects the latest changes. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
